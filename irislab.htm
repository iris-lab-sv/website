Short Bio

Younghee Park joined Computer Engineering at San Jose State University in August 2013. She is also serving as Executive for STCCS. She selected Kordestani Endowed Chair in the College of Engineering in 2016. Before joining this department, she was a post-doctoral researcher in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2013 and at Columbia University in New York City in 2011. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science from North Carolina State University in 2010. She worked in National Security Research Institute at ETRI in Korea in 2003. Her primary research interests are in network and system security. Her research has been funded by many industries and National Science Foundation (NSF).
